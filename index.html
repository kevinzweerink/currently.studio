Case Study
Control Shift

  • User research
  • Digital product design
  • Design systems

Control Shift offers software to activist organizations that helps them mobilize international audiences to influence policy and raise awareness around critical issues. We helped them overhaul their admin tools, bringing order and clarity to a dashboard that had grown organically over the life of the company.

A year after the project wrapped up, the founder of Control Shift sent an email acknowledging our work on their product.

The style guidance and reusable patterns you put together all of that time ago are still relevant and useful to the team—even though we've shipped most of that work, and are on to other things.

Control Shift's product is used by organizations in over 25 countries, including 350.org, United We Dream, and Color of Change.
