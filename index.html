Currently is a studio focused on interactive storytelling and digital editorial design.

We work with publishers, tech companies, and cultural institutions to build surprising new ways of telling important stories.

  • Art direction and layout for the Fall issue of Tribune, one of the UK’s oldest political magazines.
  • Prototyping and building an experimental publication documenting Year One at Fikra Campus.
Previously

kevin@currently.studio

Rooted in the idea of the web as a medium for communication and the exchange of knowledge, Currently makes work that prioritizes clarity while challenging the conventions of digital media.

Currently is the studio practice of Kevin Zweerink. Previously, Kevin has had the pleasure of working in various full-time and freelance capacities with The New York Times, Friends of The Web, Tribune, and others. He’s instructed in the Graphic Design department at MICA, and served as Designer-in-Residence at Fikra Campus.
