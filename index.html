Currently is a new studio focused on interactive storytelling and digital editorial design.

We work with publishers, technology companies, and cultural institutions to build surprising new ways of telling engaging stories.

Currently
  • Working with Columbia Journalism Review on data-driven stories for print and the web.
  • Art direction and layout for the Fall issue of Tribune, the UK's oldest political magazine.
  • Prototyping and building an experimental digital publication documenting Year One at Fikra Campus.
Previously
  • NTYPE, a typeface in the fourth dimension.
  • Drip Drop, an iOS timer to help reliably brew good pour over coffee.
  • Brannon.online, a portfolio site for an artist, programmer, and researcher.

Currently is the practice of Kevin Zweerink.

Before Currently, Kevin has had the pleasure of working in various full-time and freelance capacities with The New York Times, Friends of The Web, Tribune, and others. He's instructed in the Graphic Design department at MICA, and served as Designer-in-Residence at Fikra Campus. Currently, he lives and works in Brooklyn, NY.
