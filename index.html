Currently is a studio focused on interactive story­telling and digital editorial design.

We work with publishers, tech companies, and cultural institutions to build surprising new ways of telling important stories.

  • Prototyping and building an experimental publication documenting Year One at Fikra Campus.
Previously

kevin@currently.studio

Rooted in the idea of the web as a medium for the exchange of ideas, Currently makes work that puts clear communication first while challenging conventions of digital media.

Currently is the studio practice of me, Kevin Zweerink. Previously, I’ve had the pleasure of working in various full-time and freelance capacities with The New York Times, Friends of The Web, Tribune, and others. I’ve instructed in the Graphic Design department at MICA, and served as one of the first Designers-in-Residence at Fikra Campus.

This site is built in plain old HTML & CSS, and hosted on Github Pages. The typeface used is Monument Grotesk Semi-Mono by Dinamo.
